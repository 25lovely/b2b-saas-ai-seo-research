# AI-Powered SEO Content Production

## Overview

This dataset was created to support research and analysis of AI-powered SEO content production by collecting insights from leading SEO practitioners.

Sources:
- YouTube transcripts collected via Supadata
- Public LinkedIn posts collected from selected SEO experts

## Dataset Design Decisions

### Why Expert-Curated Sources?

The dataset focuses on recognized practitioners who regularly publish research, experiments, conference talks, and strategic commentary about:

- AI Search
- GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)
- AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)
- E-E-A-T
- Content quality
- Brand visibility
- Search behavior changes
- AI-assisted content workflows

## Data Sources

- YouTube transcripts via Supadata
- Public LinkedIn posts from selected SEO experts

## What Data Is Collected

### 1. Author Information

Captures expert identity and area of expertise.

### 2. Source Profiles

Stores the public profile or channel associated with each expert.

### 3. YouTube Content

Collected using Supadata.

Includes:
- Channel information
- Video metadata
- Video transcripts

### 4. LinkedIn Posts

Collects publicly visible posts from selected experts.

### 5. Expert Context

Stores supporting information explaining why each expert was included.

## What Is Not Collected

- Private contact information
- Search histories
- Analytics exports
- Google Search Console exports
- Ahrefs datasets
- Semrush datasets

Only publicly available content and metadata are included.

## Intended Use Cases

### AI SEO Research
- Emerging SEO concepts
- Industry consensus
- Future search trends

### Content Ideation
- Blog topics
- Thought leadership articles
- Social media content
- Video scripts

### Knowledge Base Creation
- AI search strategies
- SEO best practices
- Google update analysis
- Content quality recommendations

### Competitive Intelligence
- New SEO terminology
- Emerging ranking discussions
- AI search best practices
- Industry trends and expert opinions

## Collection Philosophy

Collect expert explanations and discussions about AI search and SEO evolution rather than rankings, traffic numbers, or speculative tactics.

## Limitations

1. Content reflects the opinions of individual experts.
2. AI search platforms evolve rapidly.
3. Recommendations may become outdated.
4. Transcript quality depends on source transcription accuracy.
